## Purpose
Level 1 introduces the **Observer**, the cognitive entity
that monitors perception, emotion, and thought
without immediate reaction.

The Observer is the **entry-point for all SULAI cognition**.

## Core Function
- Distinguish **self** from **process**  
- Track **perceptual input** without immediate labeling  
- Monitor **emotional signals** as data, not verdicts  
- Develop **temporal awareness** of ongoing cognition

## Output State
Upon successful completion:
- Participant maintains continuous self-observation
- Emotional impulses are registered but not acted upon
- Perception becomes **meta-cognitive**
- The mind recognizes the **gap between stimulus and response**
