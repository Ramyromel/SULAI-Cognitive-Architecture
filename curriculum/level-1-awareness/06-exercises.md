# Level 1 — Awareness Exercises

## Exercise 1: Silent Observation
- Sit in a neutral environment for 15–30 minutes
- Note every sensory input without labeling
- Record only raw perceptions in your internal "ledger"

## Exercise 2: Emotional Logging
- Monitor emotions as they arise
- Avoid reaction; note onset, intensity, duration

## Exercise 3: Temporal Gap Training
- Before responding to any stimulus, count 3–5 internal beats
- Evaluate awareness of the pause

## Exercise 4: Observer Journaling
- Document subjective experience without conclusions
- Highlight patterns, not narratives

**Success Criteria:**  
- Ability to remain present without narrative for extended periods  
- Recognition of emotional impulses without automatic reaction
