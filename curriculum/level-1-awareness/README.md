## Purpose

Level 1 establishes the **Observer** as a distinct cognitive function.
The Observer is not a thinker, judge, or decision-maker.
It is a stabilized reference point that *witnesses* perception,
emotion, and internal cognitive activity without interference.

This level is mandatory for all subsequent SULAI stages.
No emotional modeling, temporal reasoning, or ethical evaluation
is valid without a coherent Observer.

---

## Cognitive Objective

The learner must demonstrate the ability to:

- Detect internal cognitive events without modifying them
- Maintain perceptual continuity across changing stimuli
- Separate observation from interpretation
- Recognize attention drift in real time

Awareness here is **non-reactive** and **pre-decisional**.

---

## Core Concepts Introduced

- Observer vs Thought
- Awareness without narrative
- Attention anchoring
- Perceptual continuity
- Cognitive noise detection

These concepts are expanded formally in the module files.

## Completion Criteria

Progression from Level 1 requires:

- Demonstrated observer continuity
- Absence of compulsive interpretation
- Stable attention window
- Verifiable awareness of awareness itself

Failure to meet these conditions **blocks advancement** by design.

---

## Architectural Note

Level 1 does not produce knowledge.
It produces **cognitive positioning**.

Without a stable Observer,
all higher cognitive layers become distorted.
