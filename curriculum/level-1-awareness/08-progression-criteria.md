# Level 1 — Progression Criteria

Progression to Level 2 (Feeling) requires:

- Continuous Observer activity maintained for ≥30 minutes/session
- Emotional impulses recognized before action
- Sensory data logged without narrative
- Ability to detect cognitive drift and self-correct

## Verification
- Self-verification mandatory
- No external authority can certify this stage

Upon stabilization, the Observer is ready for **Level 2 — Feeling**.
